Reemus does Unity

So I've been trying to accomplish small goals every day, one thing that had been eluding me up until now was getting Unity into this squarespace layout. After a bit of Google sleuthing I finally found the solution, so here's Reemus inside the Unity engine for the first time ever. This may seem like nothing but it took me a couple months to learn all the little pieces to make Reemus walk around this room. The cursor is a new addition, it's going to give you different options when you scroll over a hotspot. Which is a big difference from the strict one click design of the other games. I thought giving the option to "look at" something would give more opportunity for jokes and hints, so I'm trying it out.

So the plan now is to get chunks of the game finished and beta test them in the browser like this. I also received an email from Bill D. who suggested I start you tubing the process again, so I'm going to do that too. In the meantime, make Reemus walk around his room. (I need a higher res export of the BG, looks a little fuzzy on here.)

EDIT - I removed the file, it worked great at first but something went wacky and I couldn't load it without my browser crashing. Not sure if worked for anyone else. Back to the drawing board!
